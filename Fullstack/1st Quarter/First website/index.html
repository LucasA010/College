Origin

Pixar originated in the 70s when Ed Catmull was hired by Lucasfilm to improve the graphic tecnulogy at that time. Ed's division developed the Pixar Image Computer with the ability to render high quality 3D culor images.

Just in 1986 the division became indepent and thus originating Pixar which was acquired by Steve Jobs, with the intent of merchandising Pixar's computer.

In 1989, through and agreement with Disney, Pixar started to work in Toy Story, becoming than the company that we all know nowadays.
